Title: A Short Story of UNISWAP and UNI Token. DEFI Explained

1. Uniswap is a protocol for decentralized exchange of tokens on the Ethereum blockchain.
2. Uniswap is built on the concept of liquidity pools and automated market makers.
3. The initial version of Uniswap was published to the Ethereum mainnet on the 2nd of November 2018.
4. Uniswap v2 was launched in May 2020, which added ERC20-ERC20 liquidity pools and other features.
5. Uniswap's trading volume started picking up in May 2020 and grew exponentially in August 2020 due to the yield farming craze.
6. SushiSwap came into play at the end of August 2020, aiming to compete with Uniswap by forking the project and stealing its liquidity.
7. Uniswap announced the launch of their new token, Uni, on the 16th of September 2020.
8. Everyone who had used Uniswap even once before the 1st of September was eligible to claim their 400 Uni tokens.
9. The Uni tokens were distributed to around 50,000 Ethereum addresses, making them one of the most widely distributed tokens in the space.
10. Uniswap's Uni token was launched to further decentralize the protocol and make it a publicly owned and self-sustainable financial infrastructure.
